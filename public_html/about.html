About Us

Small Pharaoh has been serving delicious and authentic Middle Eastern cuisine for over 10 years. Our food truck, located at the Midtown Beer Garden in Portland, OR, has been a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Recently, we have expanded our operations with a new restaurant in Vernonia, OR, offering indoor seating and an enhanced menu.
